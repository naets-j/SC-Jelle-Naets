LED Visuals · Show Direction · Co-Founder

BLEETFOEF MIDNIGHT SHOWS

I'm the co-founder of Bleetforce NPO, the organizing body behind Bleetfoef, a Belgian Drum & Bass rave concept that has been making waves over the past five years. With a growing international community of 20.000+ fans across social media, we strive to do better every time.

By creating unique storylines behind each event, we turn every rave into an experience like no other. We design everything to create unforgettable moments with top-tier productions, one-of-a-kind midnight shows, delicious food and interactive elements.

While being responsible for the creative and audiovisual direction of these midnight shows, I design LED visuals that breathe life into the visual experience on stage.

Logo Renders · VDV × Bad Habitz

VDV JUNGLE GOD

I collaborated with VDV and Bad Habitz Festival on the very first 100% VDV set, featuring a fully integrated audiovisual show. The concept was to represent the DJ as an ancient jungle god, drawing inspiration from Mayan mythology and ritualistic visuals. I developed a series of custom 3D logo animations designed to enhance the artist's live performance throughout the entire show.

How I work
MY WORKFLOW
01
STEP 1.
CONCEPT &
DIRECTION

Every project starts with defining the visual identity and overall direction, in which I explore different ideas through references, moodboards and early sketches. Whether it's for live shows, brands or digital content, this phase is all about shaping a clear concept, setting the foundation for everything that follows.

02
STEP 2.
3D
DEVELOPMENT

Once the direction is clear, I start translating the concept into 3D. This involves building environments, developing compositions, testing lighting and experimenting with different visual approaches. Throughout this process, I resolve any issues and ensure the visuals not only look strong, but also communicate the intended mood or message.

03
STEP 3.
FINAL
VISUALS

In the last stage, I refine the details and finalize the renders or animations. I focus on a polished result that captures attention and leaves a lasting impression whilst fully working within its intended context.
